## 📌 Project Overview
An executive-level Business Intelligence (BI) and Data Analytics project that consolidates **114+ Million impressions** across multiple multi-year marketing campaigns (**2017 - 2020**). This project transforms disparate marketing tables (Ad Spend, Click Costs, Impression Costs, and Conversions) into an interactive, insight-driven dashboard designed to optimize budget allocation, track conversion funnels, and maximize Return on Investment (ROI).

The ultimate goal of this project is to bridge the gap between tactical marketing metrics (Clicks, Impressions, CTR) and corporate financial performance (Gross Revenue, Cost of Goods Sold (COGS), Gross Profit Margin, and Sales Target Attainment).

---

## 🚀 Key Business Results & Impact
By analyzing the historical data across 3 major ad campaigns and 4 distribution platforms, the analysis uncovered several strategic performance indicators:

* **Total Revenue Generated:** **$113.33M** across a 4-year lifecycle.
* **Production & Scale:** **$35.84M** in Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) with **456,921 units sold** worldwide.
* **Platform Dominance:** **Platform A** emerged as the primary revenue driver, generating **$43.27M** (38.2% of total revenue) and leading all channels with over **173.7K conversions**.
* **Campaign Optimization:** **Campaign 3** was identified as the highest performing marketing initiative, yielding **$45.36M** in sales, outpacing Campaign 1 ($36.83M) and Campaign 2 ($31.14M).
* **Audience Engagement Hub:** **Platform D** established itself as a highly competitive runner-up, generating **$31.28M** in revenue via **126.1K conversions**.

### Core Analysis Pillars Built Into the Dashboard:
1.  **Executive KPI Cards:** Real-time visibility into Gross Revenue, Total Volume Sold, Total Clicks, and End-to-End Funnel Conversion Rates.
2.  **Temporal Performance vs. Targets:** Interlocking historical timelines mapping monthly sales against strict corporate **Sales Targets** and **Ad Budgets**.
3.  **Ad-Type Mix Distribution:** Granular breakdowns comparing the volume and engagement across different creative formats: *Display Ads, Overlay Ads, Skippable Ads, Non-skippable Ads,* and *Bumper Ads*.
4.  **Platform Efficiency Matrix:** Interactive components detailing user behavior indicators per platform, such as **Average Pages Visited** and **Average Time on Site (mins)**, directly correlated to conversion outcomes.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Demonstrated
* **Data Modeling & Warehousing:** Fact-Dimension star schema modeling, linking the core performance ledger with dynamic rate dimensions (`Click Cost`, `Impression Cost`, `Ad Budget`, and `Sales Target`).
* **Advanced ETL Processing:** Extracted, cleaned, and merged structured `.csv` / `.xlsx` multi-table sources using Power Query / Python (Pandas) to eliminate data redundancies.
* **Business Intelligence & UI/UX:** Designing executive dashboards with focused visual hierarchies, custom color palettes, and frictionless user filter paths (slicers).
* **Financial Analytics:** Calculating complex calculated fields for profit margins, cost per acquisition (CPA), click-through rate (CTR), and budget variance analysis.

## 📐 Data Schema & Architecture
The project structure is driven by a comprehensive analytical engine built out of 5 primary data matrices:

* `Data Engine Matrix`: Captures chronological ad formats, engagement footprints (Clicks, Impressions), web traffic behavior, and unit economics.
* `Impression Cost Dimension`: Dynamically adapts historical Cost Per 1000 Impressions (CPM) variations broken down by platform and year.
* `Click Cost Dimension`: Houses historical fluctuating Cost Per Click (CPC) indicators.
* `Ad Budget Registry`: The target benchmark representing baseline marketing caps by month.
* `Sales Target Registry`: The operational milestone matrix evaluating performance against corporate sales projections.

## 🔍 Deep-Dive Insights & Strategic Recommendations
* **Double Down on Platform A:** Platform A has proven to have the highest intent audience, maintaining leadership in both volume and gross conversions. Marketing budgets should be skewed 15-20% more toward Platform A in subsequent quarters.
* **Scale Campaign 3 Frameworks:** Because Campaign 3 systematically generated the highest ROI and top-line revenue, its creative mix strategies (ad types, messaging themes) should be reverse-engineered and applied to future campaigns.
* **Optimize Low-Performing Channels:** Platform B generated the lowest revenue ($16.06M) and conversion rates. An audit of user behavior (Average Time on Site) should be done to address potential leaks in the Platform B landing page funnel.
